What is the Kolin platform?

The Kolin platform was created for becoming a bridge between different cultures, languages and also to ease access to education. Thus, Kolin platform will become an agent of change by creating the first decentralized centre for translation and information exchange. Multilingual and open technical material will be generated within a social branch of the platform, and a private branch will offer translation services to private entities. Working both in parallel in order to make access to knowledge universal, making Kolin a core point for the generation of a more inclusive world.

How will the Kolin token work?

Our coin built on a Waves smart contract platform is an open source community based cryptocurrency which would work on a peer-to-peer decentralized mechanism. It gives users from any part of the world a chance to become independent and have access to translation services (documents, articles, websites, etc.) to a variety of languages, by using the Kolin currency for paying to specialised translators via the blockchain, shortening the language barriers within an user-friendly environment.

Kolin tokens are valid indefinitely and are the property of their respective holders

What is special in the Kolin sale mechanics?

KOLIN will be sold in an Initial Public Offering (IPSO) format, using a standard an transparent procedure commonly used in stocks exchange, with the added benefits that blockchain grants. The sale will be performed on a completely Public format where everyone can track the procedure of this one. The total supply on this IPSO is 1 billion, distributed on ascending steps of 200 wavoshis and 100M Kolin. This will work on a “first come first served” format. Further information in FAQ section.

How is the community involved in the Kolin project?

Community is one of the strongest aspects for the kolinplatform development and functionality. As Kolin is an inclusive project, people from all over the world is invited to participate in the technical liabilities of Kolin. Additionally, the Kolin platform will contain a social branch that will allow any person to register in order to help with the translation of information from a technical to a more generic language, allowing common users to have access to technical knowledge in a more understandable format.

FAQs


Kolin Frequently Asked Questions.

How can I acquire Kolin?

You can get Kolin by paying with Waves and Bitcoin.

Steps for buying through the Waves DEX platform :
1) Enter https://wavesplatform.com/ and open the online client
2) Enter your password or create a new wallet
3) Save and secure your keystore file
4) Next type Kolin on the Waves or BTC search box
5) There you can track Kolin price and how much has been sold so far
6) Just buy Kolin using Waves or BTC, be aware of the m.a.p.w for participating on the bonus bounty #Check exchanges section for information about centralised exchanges

What made Kolin sale different from other ICOs?

KOLIN will be sold in a novel Initial Public Sale Offering (IPSO) format, using a standard and transparent procedure that everyone can track. This worked on a “first come first served” format.

Furthermore, as Kolin tokens are valid indefinitely and are the property of their respective holders, Kolin were immediately available for trading after purchased.

On which exchanges Kolin can be traded?

Kolin was initially traded on the Waves decentralised exchange (DEX) which allows trading different assets back and forth between users, as a traditional exchange, with stronger security guarantees to end users due to its decentralised nature. Waves DEX has a trade volume of USD$1 million.

Kolin is also available on centralized exchanges as Crex24 and Kompler exchange. Other centralized exchange are also being considered

How did the IPSO work and what was the total supply offered?

1 billion Kolin were offered on this IPSO, distributed on ascending steps of 200 wavoshis and 100M Kolin. This worked on a “first come first served” format.


First owners of Kolin received different bonuses after finalized each sale stage, as detailed next:

1st stage: 100M Kolin at 200 wavoshis ; owners received + 50% bonus*
2nd stage: 100M Kolin at 400 wavoshis; owners received + 25% bonus*
3rd stage: 100M Kolin at 600 wavoshis; owners received + 10% bonus*
4th stage: 100M Kolin at 800 wavoshis; owners received + 5% bonus*
5th stage: 100M Kolin at 1000 wavoshis; owners received + 2.5% bonus*

*m.a.p.w. = 500K Kolin was the maximum airdrop received per wallet

Is my personal information protected with Kolin?

As Kolin sale is made in an open decentralised environment, you do not need to provide any personal information, making the sale private and confidential. Even though, your information will never be shared or sold to anyone.

Why did you offer a bounty bonus with m.a.p.w.?

As Kolin’s main goal is to shorten barriers between different languages and cultures, we wanted to benefit as much people as possible with the initial low prices offered. Hence, using m.a.p.w helped to restrict the accumulation of large quantities of Kolin during the IPSO.



For instance, an investor needed to buy a maximum of 1M Kolin on the first stage to receive the maximum reward of 500K. However, if that same person wanted to receive more rewards, it needed to wait until the next sale stage, where a holder needed 2M Kolin for receiving the maximum reward.
